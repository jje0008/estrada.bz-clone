Origin, history and coats of arms, 
also known as shields or crests, of 
Spanish - Hispanic surnames.

Do you want to know the origin, primitive history and coat of arms
of your Spanish - Hispanic Surname?

In the Web there are many sites that provide this service, offering elaborate certificates which, generally, don't contain either the real origin of the surname, its real primitive history or its original coat of arms. Many of those interested in their surname origins have fallen victim to these commercial stratagems and have paid good money for worthless information that is mostly invented or badly researched, even though it may look pretty. 

In this page we offer those who may be interested in well researched information on the origin and primitive history of their Spañish surname, data from the best genealogical sources in Spain, such as the brothers Garcia-Carraffa, Francisco Piferrer, Vilar y Pascual and others. The result is a deep study of the surname as well as its original coat of arms.

For those who may have an interest and request it, we'll send you a small sample of the text as well as a sample of the coat of arms in low dpi. If you wish to have the complete information and the coat of arms in high definition, we'll send them for a modical fee.

Those interested may contact the Webmaster at eduardo_j_estrada@yahoo.com
