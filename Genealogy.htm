Genealogy and Branches of Estrada in the World.

The ceiba tree at the right shows graphically a family tree, beginning with the roots, the trunk, the primary branches and those derived from them.
In the case of the Estradas, the trunk is formed by those who adopted as their surname the place where they lived, which was a tower in an "estrada" where many roads met.
